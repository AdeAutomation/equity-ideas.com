Study Abroad

This program is designed to help students and their families seek admission into colleges abroad. Each step is personalized to help the student find the "best fit" college that will meet the needs and priorities of the students, and to demonstrate their uniqueness in the application process. We counsel students through personality and career assessments, organize and assist them in creating a timeline of the college process, develop an appropriate college list, provide specific help with course selection, applications, essays, interviews, and preparation for visas

For accountability, any organizations must have clear, concise, relevant and easily understood methods of evaluating the employees at all levels and in all roles. We use innovative techniques of evaluating teachers for better performance in a teaching and learning environment.
